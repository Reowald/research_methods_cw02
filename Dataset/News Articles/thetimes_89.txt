I’ve been driving my electric car for more than eight years now and still firmly believe that their widespread adoption is critical to our goal as a country of achieving net zero.

That’s why it was so disappointing when the government pushed the ban on the sale of new internal combustion engine vehicles back five years to 2035. It sent out completely the wrong message. Time is most certainly not on our side, and having a realistic, achievable target (which 2030 was) to set as a clear target is important.
