At one time, Prince Andrew had it all. Not just the Queen's favourite son, he was a naval officer and decorated war hero whose dashing looks made him the 'Playboy Prince' - one of Britain's most eligible men. Recently, however, the Duke of York has become better known for one of the most extraordinary falls from grace ever experienced by a member of the Royal Family . Today, he turns 64.
