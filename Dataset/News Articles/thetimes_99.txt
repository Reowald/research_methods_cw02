What price, at auction, that bespoke Paris Saint-Germain shirt the club designed for Kylian Mbappé 15 months ago? It has the player’s surname across its back, alongside the number 2025. It was tailored to commemorate Mbappé signing a three-year deal to keep him in Paris. Only there was no asterisk, no small print on the sleeves or cuffs explaining that the third year, 2024-25, was strictly optional.

PSG have now made public that they are reconciled to Mbappé not choosing the option of a third season and there can be no stopping him joining Real Madrid, out of contract, this summer. It makes that 2025 shirt and all the photos of it look suddenly like a taunt at PSG, a relic on a timeline of
