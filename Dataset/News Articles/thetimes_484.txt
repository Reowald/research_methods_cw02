Duncan Eastland is building a home that generates more solar power than any other in Britain. His house is the first in the UK to be certified Passivhaus Premium, the platinum standard for low-energy buildings, yet you couldn’t tell by looking at it.

“I wanted somebody walking by on the footpath not to know that there was anything super-eco about the house,” Eastland says. “I wanted it to look like a really cool but normal house.” And it does.
