JamJar

Rose A3 print, £195

Inspired by the garden and woods that surrounded the cofounder Melissa Alexander’s childhood home in Sussex, JamJar elevates the simple charm of local wild flowers. For Valentine’s Day they’re offering their usual array of gorgeous seasonal flowers as well as a special collection of pressed rose artworks framed in white. Picked and pressed in the late summer, these are the more sustainable way to buy and enjoy roses and are the perfect gift for an eco-conscious loved one.
