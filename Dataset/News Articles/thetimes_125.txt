A confession before we start. Last week I took a bootful of rubbish to our local “dump”. The way it works, you have to put rubble, fridges, paint pots and car batteries in the first section and everything else — wood, garden waste, metal and nonrecyclables (the shame) — in the second section. You can drive from section one to section two but not back again. No sirree, they’re very strict about that.
