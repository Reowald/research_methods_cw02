Matt Smith has said he agreed with Ralph Fiennes about trigger warnings in the theatre

Art is meant to be dangerous and designed to shock, says Doctor Who actor

Matt Smith has agreed that trigger warnings in theatre should be scrapped, as he suggested that art is “meant to be dangerous”.

The British actor, best known for his roles in Doctor Who and The Crown, was speaking on BBC One’s Sunday With Laura Kuenssberg when he was asked about comments made on the show last week by Ralph Fiennes, who had said that theatre audiences should be “shocked and disturbed”.
