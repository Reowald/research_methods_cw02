Susanna Reid has rejected calls for her Good Morning Britain co-host Richard Madeley to be sacked after asking controversial question on Israel-Hamas conflict. In October, Madeley was forced to apologise after asking British-Palestinian MP Layla Moran if there was 'any word on the street' ahead of Hamas's terror attack on Israel. Reid has now defended her Good Morning Britain co-host, telling the Sun that Madeley is a 'bit bolder with his wording' but be is a 'legend in broadcasting'. She said: 'He's the founder of This Morning, he's been doing it longer than any of us and he's got a particular way with politicians. Layla Moran dealt with that with enormous grace and she wasn't offended.' ...read
