Junya Ito, 30, has been kicked out of the Japan team at the Asia cup over a sexual assault allegation, The Japan Football Association confirmed. Ito, who plays for the French club Stade de Reims, was staying in Doha with Japan's national team for matches at the Asian Cup tournament. The midfielder is accused of engaging in unwelcome sexual conduct with two women, who had been drinking, at a hotel in Osaka in June 2023.
