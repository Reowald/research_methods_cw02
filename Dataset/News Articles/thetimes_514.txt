Generative AI dominated headlines in 2023. The technology, showcased by the launch of OpenAI’s super intelligent chatbot ChatGPT, wowed the world with its ability to write essays, code and even poetry on a par with most humans. It amassed more than one hundred million users in a mere two months, making it the most successful product launch in history.

However, it remains to be seen if generative AI can attract a more valuable user base than the world’s homework-evading students. Will the column inches convert into corporate contracts this year? Or is it back to business as usual for the world of enterprise?
