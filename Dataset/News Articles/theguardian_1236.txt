Group of Hassidic Jewish men stand on Clifftown Parade looking out over the River Thames, 2022 ‘Sometimes I go out with my camera and nothing presents itself and other days I’ll go out and it feels as if pictures are waiting to be made at every turn. One day I was sitting in my front room and a group of Hassidic Jewish men were walking past on a cold January afternoon. I knew it would make for an interesting image with the juxtaposition of their black attire against the backdrop of the vibrant sky and sea. I captured this image as they looked over the water towards Kent’
