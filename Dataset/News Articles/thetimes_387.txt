John Tavener, who died in 2013, would have been 80 this year. Fitting, then, that the Britten Sinfonia, led from the violin by Thomas Gould, is touring his most celebrated orchestral work, The Protecting Veil, which was premiered in 1989 and then achieved extraordinary record sales in an era when “holy minimalism” was briefly all the rage. The Britten Sinfonia programme is at the Barbican, Feb 15, and Bridgewater
