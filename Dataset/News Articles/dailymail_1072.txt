Lavish parties, grand estates, rubbing shoulders with world leaders... it's not unusual for aristocrats to have opulent Instagram profiles showing them living the high life and travelling the world. The likes of Lady Kitty Spencer, Emma Weymouth and Mary Charteris have racked up hundreds of thousands of followers thanks to their gorgeous looks, opulent lives and friendships with A-listers. But often missing from many socialites social profiles is their husbands. Speaking to FEMAIL, former butler to King Charles III, now a British etiquette expert, Grant Harrold said this is nothing new.'There's images sometimes of maybe family, husbands, but the accounts are mainly set up for them to show off their lifestyle. What you've got to remember about aristocracy, is that these ladies, when they marry into the family, they have their own lives as such,' he explained. ...read
