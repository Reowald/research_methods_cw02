EXCLUSIVE: Properties in Sulgrave, located eight miles south of Newcastle, recently plummeted by 53.5 percent. Startling figures showed the average property price plunged from £73,270 to £34,074 in the last year. Residents say crime in the area is the main cause and even claimed incidents have got so frequent a local flat's living room is used by police as a holding cell.
