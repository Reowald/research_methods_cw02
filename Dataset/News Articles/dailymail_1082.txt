Liza Tarbuck broke down in tears yesterday reading out a listener's tribute to Steve Wright on his last ever Sunday Love Songs show - as they revealed their sadness that they would never be able to dance to his musical picks again. Presenting a special episode show yesterday morning, Tarbuck, 59, said the show was going on but 'without the chief'. She also read out heartfelt messages from listeners in memory of the much-loved DJ.
