Ian J Stones, who has worked with big US firms including General Motors and Pfizer , was jailed for five years after being convicted in 2022. The business executive's daughter, Laura Stones, said that he has not confessed to the crime, but added that her father 'has stoically accepted and respects that under Chinese law he must serve out the remainder of his sentence.' Neither she nor the British Embassy has been permitted to see legal documents relating to the case, the Wall Street Journal reports.
