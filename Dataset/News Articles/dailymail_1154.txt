Spain's acting Prime Minister Pedro Sanchez has provoked widespread anger over his pledge of amnesty for Catalan separatists. Sanchez received support of the National Basque Party (PNV) and the Canaries' Coalition, along with that of Catalan separatist party Junts. As a result, he looks assured of another term in office after securing the backing of the regional parties - giving him an absolute majority in the 350-member lower house in a vote due to take place in the coming days.
