British police believe that the chef accused of selling 1,200 packages of poison to people with suicidal thoughts posed as a doctor on a forum for people experiencing mental health crisis and then directed them to his online shop. Kenneth Law is accused of setting up an account on the forum and naming himself 'Greenberg,' then posing as a retired New York medical examiner.
