Morpeth, Northumberland

This detached brick house has four bedrooms built into the eaves and sits on the Purdy’s Stream section of the River Wansbeck in this market town. The property is built to make the most of the views from as many rooms as possible and from the patio garden. The walled garden has been designed for entertaining with a hut decked out as a bar, complete with a sitting room area, TV, bar stools and exposed ceiling beams. It is a 20-minute walk to the station, where you can travel to Newcastle in about 15 minutes. £595,000. S anderson Young , 07523 696 041
