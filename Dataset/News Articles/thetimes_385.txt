‘I was guided by the music,” Mike Scott says. The lead singer of the Waterboys is resplendent in a natty blue suit and cowboy hat as he recalls the making of This Is the Sea. The album is celebrated in a six-CD box set, 1985, that documents its creation, and it is also being reissued on vinyl.

In the mid-1980s the Waterboys were roped in with other “big music” bands such as Simple Minds and U2. “I didn’t have anything against those bands, but I never felt like them,” Scott says. “I was coming from a different place. My music began with an acoustic guitar or a piano.”
