Police were called to Sea Mills in the early hours of Sunday

A woman has been arrested on suspicion of murder following the deaths of three young children at a house in Bristol.

Police were called to the address in Sea Mills, in the northwest of the city, in the early hours of Sunday when concerns were raised about the welfare of people inside the property.
