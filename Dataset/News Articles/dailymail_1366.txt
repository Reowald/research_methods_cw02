Many people, myself included, yearn for tax cuts on Budget Day. Some say reduce stamp duty. Others want the so-called 'tourist tax' abolished. Still others yearn for the end of inheritance tax. How wonderful if we could have them all! Yet, as far as I am aware, no one has been urging Mr Hunt to turn his attention to what is surely the most pressing issue of our age.
