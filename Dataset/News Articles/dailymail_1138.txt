Filmmaker Jamie Price and his partner Danielle waded into the water because the motorist 'looked like he was drowning' when they arrived at the scene in Ingatestone, Essex. Two Essex fire and rescue crews, an ambulance and a police were parked near the stricken vehicle, but Mr Price said he was shocked to see the emergency services were not doing more to help. He said: 'I asked them, "why are you not going in the water?" and they said that specific crews have different levels of training for different situations. 'They were waiting for some specialist crews, because the water level was actually 4.5ft. And they were only allowed to go in at waist height.' Mr Price said that they feared the specialised crews would not arrive in time because they were coming from Colchester, which is why they decided to take action themselves. ...read
