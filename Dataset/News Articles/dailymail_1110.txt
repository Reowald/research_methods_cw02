The dwindling number of members of the Firm that can carry out engagements is being looked upon again as King Charles steps back from public duties while being treated for cancer. While the Firm once boasted a roster of members who could carry out engagements on behalf of the King, the numbers have shrunk as Charles 'slimmed down the monarchy'. With Prince and Princess of Wales also out of action while Kate recovers from surgery, the brunt of the work will be taken by Queen Camilla, Princess Anne, Prince Edward and the Duchess of Edinburgh.
