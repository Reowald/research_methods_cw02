Bored of your big coat? Jaded with your jumpers? At this interminable point of winter, you are probably sick of the sight of most items the weather forecast suggests we will be wearing for a while longer yet. That’s why it’s time to work on your drapescape instead.

Here is the latest no-buy trend to make whatever you are wearing look not new but “now”, which these days is far more stylish. There were drapescapes aplenty on showgoers and street-stylers in Copenhagen last week, where there is as much sartorial inspiration to be found on the pavements as the catwalks, given its reputation as a global hotspot for staying warm and looking cool at the same time.
