Culture Secretary Angus Robertson outlined plans to set up a national broadcaster if the country leaves the UK, which would be given the legal right to show World Cup and European Championship qualifiers. However it remains to be seen how much of a pull such a plan would be. While the Scottish men's team qualified for the Euros in 2020 and again for Germany this summer, they have failed to qualify for the World Cup since 1998.
