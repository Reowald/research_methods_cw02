ALDI is recalling Carlos Takeaway Meat Feast Pasta Bake because it contains an ingredient which is not mentioned on the label and poses a possible health risk

ALDI is recalling Carlos Takeaway Meat Feast Pasta Bake because an ingredient isn't mentioned which could pose a risk to people allergic to it.

The product contains mustard but that is not mentioned on the label, meaning it's been deemed a possible health risk.

The chain warned shoppers if you have bought the product and have and allergy to mustard, do not eat it. Instead return it to the store from where it was bought for a full refund.
