Sometimes a change in the balance of power can be subtle and imperceptible, as quiet as a set of scales gently tipping from one way to another, but that is not how the Old Firm works. When one supplants the other after a long, long wait everyone is sure to hear about it. Rangers are top of the league for the first time in 18 months.

”We shall not be moved” sang their army of fans in Perth within a minute of Mohamed Diomande scoring the first half goal which put them ahead of St Johnstone and, far more importantly, Celtic. They might have gone top in either of their previous two games against Aberdeen and Ross County but could not beat either by enough
