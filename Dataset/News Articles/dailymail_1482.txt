There are health advantages of having regular cold showers, such as boosting your immunity - and I mentioned studies that show that turning down the thermostat a couple of degrees can boost levels of brown fat, a special type of fat that burns oodles of calories by generating heat. But of course, there is another, darker side to the kind of cold we're currently experiencing. Sudden cold snaps can be extremely dangerous, leading to a surge in deaths, particularly from heart disease.
