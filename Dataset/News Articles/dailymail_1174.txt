Friendly, fun and still affectionately known to most as 'Fergie', Sarah, Duchess of York , has long been the party-loving royal celebrities want to spend time with. The former-wife of Prince Andrew has remained close to her former husband while carving out a career for herself as an author, charity campaigner sometime television presenter and much else besides.
