Viewing guide, by Ben Dowell

Breathtaking

ITV1, 9pm

A drama set in a Covid ward shown over three consecutive nights? Do we really want to relive these dark times? Well, this is certainly not an easy watch but it’s an important one, written with precision, panache and some anger by Line of Duty’s Jed Mercurio, the former doctor Prasanna Puwanarajah and palliative doctor Rachel Clarke, on whose Covid memoir it draws. It’s packed with jeopardy, dramatising scenes that feel uncomfortably close to what it was like inside a Covid ward at the height of the pandemic.
