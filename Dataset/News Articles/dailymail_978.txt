Cleo Watson, nicknamed 'the Gazelle' in political circles because of her long legs, was spotted pushing a pram around the streets of North London last week after recently having her first child. The statuesque 35-year-old swapped her glamorous Westminster wardrobe for dressed-down comfort, wearing a black puffer jacket, tracksuit bottoms and trainers, and scraped her hair back in a loose bun. She smiled and chatted to a friend as they strolled, with no sign that the newborn was giving them any trouble. That must have felt a world away from her days at No 10.
