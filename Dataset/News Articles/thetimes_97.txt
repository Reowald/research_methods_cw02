When Jayne Torvill and Christopher Dean pose outside Coffee Barcelona in Sarajevo, it causes a stir among the proprietor and his customer. The two friends from Nottingham are instantly recognisable to Zahid Dzambegovic and Dragana Tiric, who pop outside for an autograph. Dzambegovic is in his late thirties, not even born when Torvill and Dean created the standout moment of the 1984 Olympics in this city, but says “everyone knows who they are”. Tiric, a teenager at the time, certainly remembers.
