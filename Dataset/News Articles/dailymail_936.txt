Matt Le Tissier has insisted he has no regrets over his controversial behaviour on social media, despite claiming it cost him his punditry job at Sky Sports. Le Tissier was axed by the broadcaster in August 2020 alongside fellow Soccer Saturday panellists Charlie Nicholas and Phil Thompson, and later blamed his dismissal on his refusal to wear a BLM badge and his views on Covid-19.
