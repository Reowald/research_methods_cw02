Anthony Joshua versus Otto Wallin is the most evenly matched fight on the card in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, on Saturday night. Wallin is a seasoned amateur and is, without a doubt, coming off a career-best win over Murat Gassiev last time out. He also gave a very good account of himself against Tyson Fury in 2019.

Fury is probably one of the most difficult heavyweights of the past two decades to come up against — his style is very frustrating, he’s a nightmare to fight — so for Wallin to face that kind of fighter and do really well, Joshua has got to approach it in an aggressive manner.
