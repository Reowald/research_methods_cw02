How do you solve the problem of a dull wardrobe? Well, if buying a whole new one is out of the question (and let’s be honest, for most of us that’s the case), it’s time to invest in a few choice wardrobe liveners. In other words bright, bold, colourful items that immediately lift an outfit and not only bring it into the new season but also create an attention-grabbing accent.

For spring, if you’ve got a classic office uniform of a smart white shirt and delightfully tailored trousers, a really bright rollneck is the thing with which to add oomph.
