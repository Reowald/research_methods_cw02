While the figures being bandied around for the sale of Wembley are mind-boggling to the average fan, in the City this type of multi-million-pound transaction is commonplace. When you sell a big asset like Wembley you first call an investment bank. You get a property valuation and factor that into a business plan then put out the word to investors and see what bids come in. You can then accept the best offer. That's how well-run businesses get value for money.
