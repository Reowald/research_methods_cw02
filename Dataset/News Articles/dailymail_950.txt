California is a state located in the western United States. It is the most populous state in the country and the third-largest by area. California is known for its diverse geography, including the Sierra Nevada mountain range, the Mojave Desert, and the Pacific coastline. The state is home to several world-renowned tourist attractions, including Disneyland, Hollywood, and the Golden Gate Bridge. California is also a hub for international trade, business, and technology, with Silicon Valley being the centre of the tech industry.
