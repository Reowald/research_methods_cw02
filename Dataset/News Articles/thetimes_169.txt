Judgment December 20, 2023

The law applicable to a claim for personal injuries by a person who had been detained by the security services of the United States in secret “black-site” facilities across the world, where he alleged he had been tortured to extract information and some of that information had been requested by the security services of the United Kingdom, was the law of England and Wales.
