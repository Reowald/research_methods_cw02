Ian Coates, Barnaby Webber and Grace O’Malley-Kumar were stabbed to death by Valdo Calocane in Nottingham last year

The families of three people stabbed to death in Nottingham last year have said they were sickened to learn police officers had shared graphic details of the injuries suffered by their loved ones in a WhatsApp group.

Barnaby Webber and Grace O’Malley-Kumar, both aged 19, and Ian Coates, 65, were killed by Valdo Calocane on the morning of June 13. Last month Calocane, 32, was convicted of manslaughter by reason of diminished responsibility and sentenced to indefinite detention in a high-security hospital.
