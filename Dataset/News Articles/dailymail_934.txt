New York Attorney General Letitia James on Friday expanded her lawsuit against crypto firms Genesis and Gemini, tripling the size of their alleged fraud scheme to more than $3 billion. The amended complaint expands an October suit James filed against Digital Currency Group, its Genesis Global Capital unit, and Gemini Trust, the exchange run by twin brothers Cameron and Tyler Winklevoss.
