The British Academy Film Awards (Today, BBC1, 7pm)

David Tennant plays host for this year’s celebration of celluloid and backslapping from London’s Royal Festival Hall. Christopher Nolan’s Oppenheimer leads the pack with 13 nominations, followed by 11 nods for Poor Things, starring Emma Stone. Scorsese’s Killers of the Flower Moon and Jonathan Glazer’s The Zone of Interest both have nine nominations in one of the strongest film line-ups in many years, while Anatomy of a Fall, The Holdovers and Maestro received seven.
