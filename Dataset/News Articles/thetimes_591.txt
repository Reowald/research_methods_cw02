Something extraordinary happened on Monday at the Robin Park Tennis Centre in Wigan, which is not a venue laden with sporting landmarks. However, here, at the Players Championship darts event, the crowd were witness not just to one nine-dart finish but three.

There was one by the shooting star Luke Littler. Another by another young star, the 18-year-old Leighton Bennett. And the third from a 50-year-old Northern Irishman, Mickey Mansell. And all in an hour.
