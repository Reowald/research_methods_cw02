One of my earliest memories is standing in Dudley High Street with my mum while she collected money for Shelter, the campaign to tackle homelessness.

In my twenties I worked for Copec which was set up to deal with the scandal of slum housing in Birmingham a century ago. After various mergers, that has grown into Midland Heart, one of the country’s leading housing associations, providing 35,000 across the Midlands.
