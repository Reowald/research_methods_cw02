Numerous reports on the BBC News website which are still visible online describe incidents in the UK and overseas as terror attacks in headlines and in the text of stories. Stories about the London Bridge, Westminster and Paris attacks all feature the words without quotation marks or attribution. The BBC has faced furious criticism over its refusal to describe the Hamas killing of around 1,300 Israelis, many of them civilians, as terrorism.
