The Business Secretary furiously denounced Henry Staunton on X last night over claims he made in a newspaper interview about his time in the role. Mr Staunton, who took up the role in December 2022 following nine years as chairman of WH Smith, suggested that Whitehall wanted to hold back compensation payments for those affected, to help the Tories ahead of the election. He also said that when Ms Badenoch sacked him in the wake of anger over the scandal she told him: 'Well, someone's got to take the rap for this.'
