President Boric describes disaster as ‘very difficult moment’ for country and says death toll likely to rise

More than 120 people have been killed by forest fires in Chile and hundreds more remain missing, according to the government, which has declared a state of emergency and two days of mourning.

The central region of Valparaiso has been the worst affected, especially the coastal tourist city of Vina del Mar. Firefighters in helicopters have struggled to reach many areas which have been blanketed by black smoke.
