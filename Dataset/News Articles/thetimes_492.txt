Oxford tops the list for the first time in 12 years

The University of Oxford has been rated the best in Britain, taking the top spot in a UK university ranking for the first time in 12 years and beating its rival Cambridge.

The Times and The Sunday Times Good University Guide 2023, published online today, places Oxford at the top of its league table of 132 universities, after the university excelled across the board in the academic measures used to compile the rankings, including demonstrating high-quality research and strong graduate job prospects.
