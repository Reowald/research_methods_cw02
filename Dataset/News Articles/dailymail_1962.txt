There's little point in bringing such a majestic device into a home where the biggest workout it's going to get is early morning Paw Patrol binges. The idea is that it's a TV with such good sound you can use it as an actual stereo. Instead of weedy speakers built into the screen, there's a rigid metal panel mounted beneath it, which contains three 4in mid-bass drivers from British hi-fi overlords Bowers & Wilkins.
